Day by day

The journey

A sample shape of the trip — final itinerary confirmed on booking.

Day 1–2

Welcome to Beirut

Arrive, settle in, and gather for a welcome dinner overlooking the Mediterranean. First evening of music and introductions.

Day 3

Byblos & the coast

One of the oldest continuously inhabited cities on earth — ancient harbour, souks, and a seaside dance session.

Day 4–5

The mountains & the Cedars

Into the highlands — Dabke, mountain villages, and the famous Cedars of Lebanon.

Day 6

Beqaa Valley & Baalbek

The monumental Roman temples, vineyards and a long table lunch in the valley.

Day 7–8

Studio intensive

Two focused days of dance with Amera and live musicians — repertoire, musicality and performance.

Day 9–10

Beirut nights & farewell

Markets, free time, and a farewell celebration with food, song and the friends you've made.

Before you book

Tour questions

Do I need to be an experienced dancer?

No. The tour welcomes all levels — sessions are tailored, and plenty of travellers come for the culture as much as the dance.

What's the deposit policy?

A $500 deposit secures your place and is refundable up to a set date. The balance is due 60 days before departure. Full terms are sent with the brochure.

Is it safe to travel to Lebanon?

We monitor official travel advice closely and plan the itinerary accordingly. We're happy to discuss current conditions when you enquire.

Can I bring a non-dancing partner?

Yes — there's a parallel cultural program, so partners and friends can join the journey too.
