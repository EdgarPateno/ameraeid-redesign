Middle Eastern dance has always been passed from master to student. A private session is a real conversation about your dance — your goals, your strengths, your habits, and what could open up for you next.

Why train privately

Deeper, more personal, more responsive

Group classes are familiar in the West, but private tuition meets you exactly where you are. Many dancers came to Amera privately to evolve professionally — here's what we focus on.

Technique

Refine the details — isolation, layering, control and the steps that make movement read on stage.

Musicality

Understand maqam as a dancer, find your flow, and let the music lead you to a true Tarab moment.

Stage presence

Present yourself with confidence and authenticity, with depth and respect for Middle Eastern audiences.

Folkloric style

Explore Baladi, Saidi, Shaabi and more for performance — knowledge that sets serious dancers apart.

Good to know

Questions, answered

Do I need experience to book?

No. Private tuition suits complete beginners through to professionals — the session is built entirely around you and your level.

Are sessions in person or online?

Both. In-person sessions are on the NSW Central Coast; online sessions run worldwide over video.

Are you running weekly group classes?

Not at present. Amera focuses on private tuition, mentoring and workshops around Australia and overseas. Private sessions give you far more individual attention than a group class.

What should I prepare?

Just come ready to move and to talk about your goals. For the Performance Intensive, bring the music or piece you're working on.
