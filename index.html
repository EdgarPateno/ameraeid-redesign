A pioneer of the art form

The woman who brought belly dance to Australia.

Amera Eid is a trail-blazing artist and entrepreneur credited with bringing Belly Dance to Sydney and Australasia — performing internationally, founding the Southern Hemisphere's first belly dance boutique, hand-beading the costume Kylie Minogue wore at the Sydney 2000 Olympics, and costuming productions including Moulin Rouge.

Beyond the stage she is a mentor, foster-care advocate and fashion designer — a life built on music, culture and human connection.
